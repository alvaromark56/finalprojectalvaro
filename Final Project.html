DARK MAGICIAN

"Dark Magician", known as "Black Magician" (ブラック・マジシャン Burakku Majishan) in Japanese, is an archetype that has been a part of the Yu-Gi-Oh! franchise since its beginning, with "Dark Magician" appearing as Yugi Muto's ace card. During the Battle City arc and subsequent arcs, more cards were introduced, such as "Dark Magician Girl". Originally a series, the release of "The Eye of Timaeus" cemented its status as an archetype. The archetype also contains a sub-archetype, "Dark Magician Girl".

ELEMENTAL HERO NEOS

"Elemental HERO Neos is one of the main cards used by Jaden Yuki in Yu-Gi-Oh! GX. It is considered the signature monster of his deck. Throughout the series, Jaden often summons Neos to fight powerful opponents. Neos is depicted as a highly versatile and powerful monster with a connection to the Neo-Spacians, a group of creatures from a parallel dimension that interact with the main universe.".

A new Elemental HERO has arrived from Neo-Space! When he intiate a Contact Fusion with a Neo-Specian his unknown powers are unleashed.
This legendary dragon is a powerful engine of destruction. Virtually invincible, very few have faced this awesome creature and lived to tell the tale.

BLUE-EYES WHITE DRAGON

"Blue-Eyes White Dragon is Seto Kaiba's most prized possession and the centerpiece of his deck. Kaiba, who is a skilled and highly competitive duelist, uses this powerful dragon to assert his dominance in duels.In the Yu-Gi-Oh! anime, Blue-Eyes White Dragon is far more than just a powerful card. It represents Seto Kaiba’s strength, ambition, and rivalry with Yugi."

ELEMENTAL HERO FLAMEWING MAN

"Elemental Hero Flame Wingman is a character and a fusion monster in the Yu-Gi-Oh! anime and trading card game. In the anime, Flame Wingman is one of the signature monsters used by Jaden Yuki, the protagonist of Yu-Gi-Oh! GX (the second series in the Yu-Gi-Oh! franchise). Flame Wingman plays a major role in Jaden's duels, particularly in his early battles.".

When this card destroys a monster by battle and sends it to the GY: Inflict damage to your opponent equal to the ATK of the destroyed monster in the GY. If you control no face-up monsters: Pay half your LP; Special Summon 1 Level 4 or lower "Elemental HERO" monster from your Deck.
When a card or effect is activated that would destroy a card(s) on the field (Quick Effect): You can Tribute this card; negate the activation, and if you do, destroy it. During the End Phase, if this effect was activated this turn (and was not negated): You can Special Summon this card from your GY.

RED DRAGON ARCHFIEND

""Red Dragon Archfiend", known as "Red Daemon" (レッド・デーモン Reddo Dēmon) in Japan, is an archetype used by Jack Atlas and Fake Jack Atlas in the Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's anime and manga as well as an alternate dimension Jack in the Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V anime series. ".

After damage calculation, when this card attacks a Defense Position monster your opponent controls: Destroy all Defense Position monsters your opponent controls. During your End Phase: Destroy all other monsters you control that did not declare an attack this turn. Monsters Summoned after your Battle Phase are excluded from this effect. This card must also remain face-up on the field to activate and to resolve this effect.
When a monster declares an attack: You can detach 1 material from this card; negate the attack. If this card is targeted for an attack, while it has no material: Destroy this card.

GALAXY-EYES PHOTON DRAGON

"Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon is a popular monster card from the Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game. It was introduced in the Photon Shockwave set (2011) and is a key card for Kite Tenjo, a character from the Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL anime series. Kite Tenjo is the main character who prominently uses Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon throughout the series. He is known for his mastery of Photon and Galaxy themed cards, with Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon being one of his signature monsters."

You can Special Summon this card (from your hand) by Tributing 2 monsters with 2000 or more ATK. During the Battle Step, if this card battles an opponent's monster (Quick Effect): You can target that opponent's monster; banish both this card from the field and that target. Return those banished monsters to the field at the end of the Battle Phase, and if you banished an Xyz Monster, this card gains 500 ATK for each material it had when it was banished.
If this card battles an opponent's monster, any battle damage this card inflicts to your opponent is doubled.

DECODE TALKER

"Yusaku uses a Cyberse Deck, with different playstyles in different seasons. In the first season, the only Extra Deck summoning method he uses is Link Summoning. He uses the Code Talkers frequently. His Signature cards are Firewall Dragon and Decode Talker. Gains 500 ATK for each monster it points to. When your opponent activates a card or effect that targets a card(s) you control (Quick Effect): You can Tribute 1 monster this card points to; negate the activation, and if you do, destroy that card.
