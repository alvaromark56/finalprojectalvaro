YU-GI-OH! SERIES

Yu-Gi-Oh! (遊☆戯☆王, Yū-Gi-Ō) is a popular Japanese franchise created by Kazuki Takahashi, which originally began as a manga series published in 1996 in Shonen Jump. It has since expanded into various media, including anime, trading card games, video games, and movies. The story of Yu-Gi-Oh! follows a young boy named Yugi Mutou, who solves an ancient Egyptian puzzle known as the Millennium Puzzle. Upon solving it, he becomes the host for the spirit of an ancient Pharaoh, who has a fragmented memory. Together, Yugi and the spirit battle opponents through a variety of games, the most notable of which is Duel Monsters — a card game central to the series.
